Go To Bed


Unreal Engine | Personal Project | 2025

Gameplay Video of Go To Bed

Description

Go To Bed is a light horror maze game where the player must find their bedroom and go to sleep before sunrise comes. The player has to shelter themself from the harsh cold exterior while keeping their mind sane before collapsing due to lack of sleep.

Role: Solo Dev Project
Tools: Unreal Engine, Blender

Level Design

One Direction Path Window

Hole in the floor path

Key and door path

Planning, 3D modeling, Maze directions
The map is inspired by a real old building where I used to live in my university town. There was a real maze of unused rooms that would have a set of doors that would only open from one direction. I translated some of that feel to the game. added a couple of elements lke keys and doors, one direction window passages and the hole on the ground.


Health System

Frost on Screen with Blue Highlight

Glitch Effect With Purple Highlight

Component for player Heat and Mind
Instead of using a normal health system, I decided on using heat and mind. The player speed is reduced as they get cold. it also has visual effects on the character mesh and visor. Mind damage triggers glitching effects on the camera. regardless, if you run out of time, heat or mind you loose the game.


Ambient change

Warm ambient effect

Cold, normal and warm areas
Depending if the player is outside, indors or near a fireplace the ambient changes to cold, normal and warm respectively. The ambient changes the music, vignettes, dirt mask, and tint of the scene.
