Gameplay Video of Glory for Goblins

Description

Raccoon’s Riddle is a point click investigation game. As a detective investigating the computer of Randy Rubbish, a raccoon whose 10-year-old daughter has been recently declared missing, Randy is currently the prime suspect. However, there is no hard evidence, so you are looking for anything on his computer. Raccoon’s Riddle was the final project of intermediate game design.
